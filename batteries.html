Our Battery Solutions

At GHTE, our Batteries Department offers an extensive range of battery solutions tailored to meet the power needs of both small-scale and large-scale applications. Whether you're powering industrial systems, commercial setups, or smaller devices, our high-performance batteries ensure reliable energy storage and supply.

In our Batteries department, we offer a wide range of battery solutions, including Valve-Regulated Sealed Lead Acid (VRLA) batteries, traction batteries, and lithium-ion batteries, catering to both small and large applications. As the sole distributors for RELIABILITY POWER SYSTEMS in the MENA region, we ensure access to high-quality products. Our factory-trained engineers and qualified staff provide comprehensive maintenance services, design solutions, testing and commissioning services, and health checks to guarantee optimal performance and longevity of your DC systems.

Our Products

As the sole distributor for RELIABILITY POWER SYSTEMS in the MENA region, GHTE provides top-quality battery solutions that are built for durability and efficiency. Our offerings include:

  • Valve-Regulated Sealed Lead Acid (VRLA) Batteries

Ideal for a variety of applications, our VRLA batteries provide maintenance-free power solutions, combining reliability with long service life.

  • Traction Batteries

Designed for heavy-duty applications, our traction batteries are built to deliver sustained energy, making them perfect for use in forklifts, electric vehicles, and industrial equipment.

  • Lithium-Ion Batteries

Offering superior energy density and fast charging capabilities, our lithium-ion batteries are perfect for modern applications where high efficiency and compact size are essential.
