Racks and Power Distribution Units (PDUs)

At GHTE, we specialize in delivering a comprehensive range of racks and Power Distribution Units (PDUs) designed to optimize your networking infrastructure. Whether for data centers or commercial environments, our solutions are built to ensure efficient organization and power management, providing a solid foundation for your IT systems.

Our Racks and PDU Solutions

Our high-quality racks and PDUs are engineered to support a variety of networking and power management needs:

  • Server Racks

Our robust server racks are ideal for housing critical equipment in both small and large-scale networks. They provide excellent airflow, cable management, and easy access to hardware components.

  • Network Racks

For more specific networking applications, our network racks offer tailored solutions for storing routers, switches, and patch panels, ensuring optimal organization and maintenance.

  • Power Distribution Units (PDUs)

Our PDUs deliver reliable power to your equipment, offering intelligent power distribution that supports efficient energy management and helps reduce downtime.

Engineering and Installation Services

Custom Design

Our team works closely with you to design rack and power solutions that fit your specific space and technical requirements.

Installation Services

We offer expert installation services to ensure that your racks and PDUs are set up correctly and function efficiently.

Maintenance and Support

To keep your system running smoothly, GHTE provides regular maintenance and support, ensuring reliable performance over the long term.
